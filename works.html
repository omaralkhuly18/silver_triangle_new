1. Web development

It is one of the favorite programming fields for the majority of programmers and it occupies the first place among programmers' jobs on World level, and there is no wonder about that, as it is an easy field to learn and is in high demand in the labor market. There is no start-up or startup company A business needs to develop its own website to prove its presence in the digital world, so the demand for... The field of web development professionals is high and continuing to grow.

Web development includes planning and designing website interfaces, and writing the software code necessary for the site to function Includes website maintenance and development as needed. The field of web development is divided into three specializations:

Frontend development
He is responsible for the visual part of the site that end users see, as he is the one who designs it Website pages and sections, menus, and determines font types, colors, etc.
Backend development
He is responsible for the technical part of the site that runs on the server using the languages PHP, MySQL , oop, java, python...etc.
Full-stack development
Or what is known as a full-stack developer, is a programmer who has... The skills that enable him to develop the front and back ends of the website. He is responsible for all... Client and server side website development aspects.

كيف تعمل؟

  • Frontend development

    It is responsible for the visible portion of the website that end users see. He is the one who designs the pages, sections of the site, and menus, and determines the types of fonts and colors...etc. This field requires an artistic sense And creative thinking to create user-friendly designs and implement them through appropriate software technologies A specialist in front-end development of websites needs to know many technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and know the most important libraries and frameworks.

  • Backend development

    He is responsible for the technical part of the site running on the server, and his responsibilities include creating functionality A main site that deals with the site's databases and monitors server performance and requires an interface developer Background: Experience with server-side languages such as Java, Ruby, PHP, and Python. It also requires familiarity with Python and database management systems such as MySQL and MongoDB Fundamentals of front-end languages and collaboration with front-end developers.

  • Full-stack development

    Or what is known as a full stack developer, is a programmer who possesses the skills that Enables him to develop the front and back ends of the website. He is responsible for all aspects of website development Client and server end.
